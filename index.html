The Big Picture

January set the tone

+20.9%
Year-over-Year Growth
+25.7%

What does +20.9% Year-over-Year Growth mean? We compare our January 2026 numbers to January 2025's numbers. This January, we grew by 20.9% compared to last year's January — meaning if we made $100 last January, we made about $121 this January. That's a big deal because January is usually a slower month after the holiday rush.

What about the +25.7% Rebound from December? December was a bit slower for us, which is normal after the Black Friday/holiday push. But instead of staying slow, January came back swinging — we grew 25.7% compared to December. That kind of bounce-back shows real momentum heading into Q1.

Bottom line: we started 2026 ahead of last year's pace. If we maintain this trajectory, we're on track to exceed 2025's full-year performance. January set the foundation — now we build on it.
